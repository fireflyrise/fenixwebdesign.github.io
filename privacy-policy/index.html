Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Fenix Web Design ("we," "us," or "our") is a home services digital marketing company that helps contractors and home service businesses across the United States grow through websites, Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and trade-specific marketing. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when you visit our website or contact us. By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions, you can reach us at mgarcia4@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

  • Information you provide: When you submit our quote request form or contact us, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, and details about the service you are interested in, such as a new website or help with Google or Facebook Ads.
  • Information collected automatically: When you browse our site, we may automatically collect standard technical data such as your IP address, browser type, the pages you visit, and the time you spend on the site, through standard web analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to run and improve our marketing services, specifically to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and free strategy call requests
  • Schedule and confirm consultations and follow-up conversations
  • Send communications related to the services you asked about
  • Improve your experience on our website

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

We only share your information in limited circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers who help us operate our website, such as hosting and analytics providers
  • When required by law or to comply with a legal process

We never sell your personal data to anyone.

You can disable or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings, though some parts of the site may not function as intended if you do.

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you submit to us. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, such as social media platforms or other external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us at mgarcia4@gmail.com and we will respond promptly.
